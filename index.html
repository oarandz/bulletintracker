How to use the Bulletin Tracker
Bulletin statuses
Pending — Bulletin not yet started
Ready — Written and ready to go
Redispatch — Being sent again with updates
Dispatched — Sent. A coloured stripe on the left shows the previous state
Breaking — Breaking news bulletin, pulses to draw attention
Clicking
🖱 Left click — Cycles through statuses (in Creation mode), or instantly Dispatches (in Delivery mode)
🖱 Right click — Opens a menu to jump to any status directly, or add/edit a note
Modes (CRE / DEL)
CRECreation — Left click cycles all statuses. For journalists building bulletins
DELDelivery — Left click goes straight to Dispatched. For presenters sending bulletins
AM / PM
Switches between the morning shift (0600–1300) and afternoon shift (1400–1900)
Notes
Right-click any bulletin and choose Add / Edit Note. A dark triangle in the top-right corner of a bulletin means a note is attached — click it to read or edit the note
Nation shared stations
A small red N in the corner of a station label means that bulletin is also shared with Nation
Countdown
The timer top-right counts down to :50 past the hour — when bulletins should be dispatched. It turns amber under 5 minutes and flashes red under 2 minutes
Reset
Resets all bulletins in the current shift to Pending and clears all notes
