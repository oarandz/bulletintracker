How to use the Bulletin Tracker
Statuses
Pending — Not yet started
Ready — Written and ready
Redispatch — Being sent again with updates
Dispatched — Sent. Coloured stripe shows previous state
Breaking — Breaking news, pulses to draw attention
Clicking
🖱 Left click (CRE mode) — Cycles through statuses
🖱 Left click (DEL mode) — Instantly dispatches
🖱 Right click — Jump to any status, or add a note
Modes
CRECreation — For journalists building bulletins
DELDelivery — For presenters; click dispatches immediately
Countdown
Counts down to your booth entry time (purple), then to the :50 deadline (teal). Based on bulletins for the upcoming hour — 2 mins each for Pending/Ready/Breaking, 10 secs for Redispatch.
Nation stations
A red N on a station label means that bulletin is shared with Nation.
Notes
Right-click any bulletin and choose Add/Edit Note. A dark triangle in the top-right corner means a note exists — click it to read or edit.
